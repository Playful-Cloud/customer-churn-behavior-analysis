\# Customer Churn Behavior Analysis



This project analyzes customer behavior and support interactions to understand drivers of churn. The goal is \*\*not prediction\*\*, but actionable insights for business intervention.



---



\## Slide 1 — Executive Overview

\*\*Purpose:\*\* Set context and outcome in under 30 seconds.  



\*\*Talking Points:\*\*

\- Our goal was to identify factors that predict customer churn and understand which groups are at highest risk.

\- This project analyzes customer behavior and support data to understand what drives churn.

\- The result is a clear risk metric and targeted recommendations to reduce churn.



---



\## Slide 2 — Business Problem \& Goals

\*\*Purpose:\*\* Explain why this matters to the business.  



\*\*Talking Points:\*\*

\- Customer churn directly impacts recurring revenue and lifetime value.

\- The goal was to identify behaviors associated with churn and define a metric leadership can monitor.



---



\## Slide 3 — Data \& Approach

\*\*Purpose:\*\* Build credibility without technical overload.  



\*\*Talking Points:\*\*

\- Reviewed all tables and datasets, checking data quality, validating data types, and identifying patterns and limitations.

\- Combined three data sources: account data, engagement events, and customer support interactions.

\- Analysis followed a structured approach: data validation, exploration, fixed data types, feature engineering, and segmentation.

\- All insights are grounded in observed data, not assumptions.
